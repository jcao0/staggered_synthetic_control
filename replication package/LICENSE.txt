Replication package license and permissions

The code and documentation in this replication package were prepared by the
authors for replication of "Synthetic Control Inference for Staggered
Adoption" and may be used for research, teaching, and replication purposes
with appropriate citation.

The empirical data included in the package are publicly available from:

Alcocer, Marco. 2024. "Replication Data for: Increasing Intergovernmental
Coordination to Fight Crime: Evidence from Mexico." Harvard Dataverse.
https://doi.org/10.7910/DVN/PBTTDM.

No restricted or confidential data are included in this package. Use of the
empirical data remains subject to the terms of the source repository.
